Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)? +

Our MOQ starts from 500 pieces for paper cups and 1,000 pieces for paper bags, depending on the product and customization requirements.

How long does production and shipping take? +

Standard production takes 10-15 days after sample approval. Shipping time varies by destination: 15-25 days by sea, 5-7 days by air.

Do you provide samples? +

Yes, we provide free blank samples. Custom printed samples require a small fee, which is refundable on your first bulk order.
